## Risk & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Scope creep | Schedule slip | Regular design reviews |
| Performance issues | Launch delay | Early profiling, optimization passes |
| Multiplayer complexity | Quality issues | Early architecture decisions |
| Content generation | Creative bottleneck | Procedural systems, template approach |
| Player retention | Revenue impact | Strong post-launch roadmap |

## Core Vision

Humanity's civilization exists aboard enormous wandering fleets called **Arks** after the collapse of intergalactic civilization. Players belong to the **Expedition Corps**, elite Drop Troopers whose mission is:

1. **Land** on hostile planets
2. **Fight** five intelligent alien races
3. **Extract** valuable resources
4. **Build** Humanity's Future

Every mission contributes to a living war where every liberated planet permanently changes the universe.

## Design Philosophy

**Movement First**: Inspired by Warframe and ULTRAKILL, movement should feel incredibly satisfying. Players can cross huge battlefields without vehicles through fluid parkour mechanics.

**Risk vs. Reward**: Like Lethal Company, extraction gameplay creates tension. Do you carry more loot or more ammunition? Large objects require teamwork.

**Living War**: Every mission matters. Planets have occupation percentages that decrease with successful operations. When a planet reaches 0% enemy control, it becomes liberated and produces resources for humanity.

**Build Diversity**: Similar to Warframe, deep customization allows players to create unique armor frames, weapons loadouts, passive modules, and abilities.
